The Shasta Ground Sloth

Nothrotheriops Shastenis is the smallest of all the giant sloths!

  • Scientific Name: Nothrotheriops Shastenis
  • Size: Up to 2.75 Metres
  • Weigth: Up to 250 Kilograms
  • Habitat: American Southwest

The Shasta Ground Sloth is an extinct species of giant sloth. In modern times they can be found as a plushie I got from the La Brea Tar Pits, on some socks an ex bought me, and in the form of a mug my girlfriend bought me.
